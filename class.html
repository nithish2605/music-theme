Sacred Traditions

Explore the divine hymns that have inspired generations

தேவாரம்

Thevaram

Thevaram refers to the first seven volumes of the twelve-volume collection of devotional poetry. These hymns were composed by three saint poets and are considered sacred literature in Shaiva Siddhanta.

7th-8th Century CE

திருவாசகம்

Thiruvasagam

A collection of hymns written by Manikkavacakar in the 9th century. These poems express intense devotion and love for Lord Shiva and are considered among the most profound works of Tamil literature.

9th Century CE

தோற்புகல்

Thoupugal

Refers to the hymns of Arunagirinathar, praising Lord Murugan. These compositions are known for their lyrical beauty, intricate rhythm patterns, and deep spiritual significance in Tamil devotional tradition.

15th Century CE
