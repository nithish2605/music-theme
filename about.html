Our Journey

2018 - The Beginning

Founded by a group of music enthusiasts and tech innovators in Los Angeles, KeshavTunes started as a passion project to solve the problem of music discovery.

2019 - First Milestone

Launched our beta platform with 100 curated artists. Reached 10,000 active users within the first three months, validating our vision for personalized music discovery.

2020 - Global Expansion

Expanded to 15 countries, partnering with local artists and labels. Introduced our innovative AI-powered recommendation engine that learns from user listening patterns.

2021 - Artist Platform

Launched Nexus Creator Studio, providing independent artists with tools for distribution, analytics, and fan engagement. Over 5,000 artists joined in the first year.

2022 - Award Recognition

Won "Best Music Innovation Platform" at the Global Tech Awards. Reached 1 million monthly active users and partnered with major festivals for exclusive content.

2023 - Present Day

Continuing to innovate with immersive audio experiences, live streaming capabilities, and community features that bring music lovers and creators closer together.
